Basic informations

Conquerist is a game on the platform Roblox. You choose a country which u want to play, and u manage it. There are a lot of options to do, for example: Build ur economy, be the best country in the world, become the king on the trade market and become the biggest country in the world! Game is still under development, u can see the sneak peaks here in updates and sneaks-peaks.
